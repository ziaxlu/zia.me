Embark on a colorful journey with Tubby Dash, an infinite runner game inspired by the nostalgic TV show "Teletubbies." Developed as my final project for my AP CSA course, Tubby Dash features an animated teletubby jumping over obstacles and collecting Tubby Toasts, with a scoreboard to keep track of top scores—all coded in Java.

Jump over teletubby/themed obstacles to collect as many Tubby Toasts as possible.

Replay endlessly to reach new heights (and more Tubby Toasts!) every play through.

View top scores by clicking the scoreboard button between rounds.
